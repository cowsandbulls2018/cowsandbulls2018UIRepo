This fun word game is a great brain teaser that you can play alone in under 5 minutes. The objective of the game is to guess a Secret Word in a limited number of tries. Based on the level selected, it can be a three, four or five letter word. For each time you try to guess, the system will return 2 clues. These 2 clues are called the Cow and the Bull.

Bull - indicates that you have guessed one of the letters in the secret word, but, it is NOT IN the correct position.

Cow- indicates that you have guessed one of the letters in the secret word, and, it is IN the correct position.

With the help of the Cows and Bulls that you uncover with each try, you are better equipped to guess the Secret Word.

This game improves logical thinking & aptitude.

keep guessing and have fun !!!
